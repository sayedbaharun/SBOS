# My Sigma Mindset — Faceless Social Media Brand

**Domain:** Media / Content
**Status:** Active
**Platform:** TikTok, YouTube, Instagram Reels

## What It Is

Faceless social media brand targeting young men. Topics: sigma mindset, self-improvement, discipline, masculinity, stoicism.

## Cowork Skills

- `sigma-content-researcher` — finds trending topics across platforms
- `sigma-script-writer` — produces spreadsheet-ready content briefs (hook, script, CTA, hashtags, thumbnail, B-roll)

## Workflow

1. Run sigma-content-researcher → outputs JSON research brief
2. Feed into sigma-script-writer → outputs complete content brief
3. Brief includes pipe-separated row ready to paste into Google Sheets
