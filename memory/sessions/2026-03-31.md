## Health Hub Redesign (Claude Code)

Rebuilt the Daily Health tab entirely. Old layout (calendar + table + insights) replaced with focused dashboard.

### What shipped
- **Hero metrics strip** — 6 large cards: Sleep | RHR | HRV | Steps | Strain | Workout. Color-coded green/amber/red, trend arrows vs 7-day average. Responsive 2/3/6-col grid.
- **Thresholds**: Sleep ≥7h=green, RHR ≤55=green, HRV ≥60ms=green, Steps ≥10k=green, Strain 10-18=green (band).
- **6 sparkline charts** — 2-col grid: Sleep (line), RHR (line), HRV (line), Steps (bar), Strain (line), Workouts (dot-bar).
- **Body Composition card** — pulls from Google Drive CSV (folder `1Oh8r-HygmAi8BrLSNsXUDvklsCyLLAW3`). Shows Weight, Body Fat %, Lean Body Mass with deltas + triple-line chart. Picks newest uploaded file by `modifiedTime desc` — safe to upload fresh CSV each week.

### Drive CSV details
- Folder: SB-OS / Knowledge Base / Health (ID: `1Oh8r-HygmAi8BrLSNsXUDvklsCyLLAW3`)
- Format: `Time, WEIGHT (kg), BODY FAT %, LEAN BODY MASS (kg)` (from Garmin/Xiaomi scale export)
- Date parsed from `Time` column (YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS → take first 10 chars)
- Zero-value rows skipped; deduplication by date (first reading of day kept)
- Server caches for 1 hour
