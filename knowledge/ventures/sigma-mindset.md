   - Output: pipe-separated row ready to paste into Google Sheets content calendar

---

## Strategic Context for Agents

- Faceless = no face on camera; visuals are stock footage, text overlays, voiceover
- Hook quality is the #1 metric — first 3 seconds determine watch rate
- Trending topics change fast; content researcher should check weekly at minimum
- Thumbnail and title are separate optimization surfaces — treat as ads
- Revenue is lagged — audience first, monetization second. Don't rush affiliate placement.
