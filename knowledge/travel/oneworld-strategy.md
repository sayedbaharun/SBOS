## Sapphire Status – Core Benefits (All Oneworld Airlines)

These apply universally across every airline listed below:

- **Lounge Access:** Business Class lounges when flying on a marketed and operated Oneworld flight
- **Priority Check-in:** Business Class check-in counters
- **Priority Boarding:** Board before general economy passengers
- **Priority Baggage:** Bags tagged and delivered first on arrival
- **Extra Baggage Allowance:** 1 additional piece or extra kg (varies by airline)
- **Preferred Seating:** Access to preferred/advance seat selection

## Baggage Rules (Detailed)

### Universal Sapphire Baggage Policy

- **Weight system routes (most international):** +15kg on top of ticketed Economy allowance
- **Piece system routes (international with domestic sectors):** 1 extra bag up to 23kg, max 2 pieces total
- **Purely domestic piece system (no ticketed baggage):** Guaranteed 1 piece up to 23kg
- **First or Business Class tickets:** Usually already include a higher allowance — Sapphire adds no further benefit on top
- **Priority baggage handling:** Yes, on all Oneworld airlines **except British Airways**

### Key Exceptions by Airline

| Airline | Exception |
|---|---|
| British Airways | No extra baggage on Hand Baggage Only / Basic fares. No priority bag handling. |
| American Airlines | Piece system applies. Extra bag only on international itineraries. No benefit on US domestic. |
| Alaska Airlines | No baggage benefit on US/Canada/Mexico-only routes |
| Qatar Airways (home) | 20kg extra OR 1 extra piece — more generous than alliance standard |

### Qatar Airways Home Airline Baggage (Gold/Sapphire tier)

- Economy: standard allowance + 20kg or 1 extra piece
- Avios can also be used to purchase additional baggage on Qatar flights
- Award ticket passengers: standard allowance only, no status baggage bonus

## Earning Avios & Qpoints

### Two Separate Currencies — Key Distinction

- **Avios** = reward currency (use to book flights, upgrades, experiences)
- **Qpoints** = status currency (used only to earn/retain tier status, not redeemable)
- Both are earned simultaneously on eligible flights

### How Avios Are Calculated on Qatar Flights

`Avios earned = Distance flown × Booking class % + Distance flown × Tier bonus %`

**Qatar Airways booking class earning rates (approximate):**

| Cabin | Booking Class | Base Earn % |
|---|---|---|
| First | F | 300% |
| Business | J, C, D, I | 150–200% |
| Premium Economy | W | 100% |
| Economy (full flex) | Y, B | 100% |
| Economy (mid) | H, K, M, L | 75–50% |
| Economy (saver/basic) | G, V, S, N | 25–50% |

**Gold tier bonus:** +75% on top of base Avios earnings on Qatar-operated flights

### Qpoints Earning (Status Points)

- Earned on Qatar-operated flights and most Oneworld partner flights
- Amount varies by airline, route, and fare class — use Qatar's My Calculator tool: qatarairways.com/qcalculator
- **Cannot be earned on award tickets** — revenue tickets only
- **Cannot be earned on Basic/Hand Baggage Only fares on some partners**

### Earning on Oneworld Partner Airlines

- Avios earned at each partner airline's own rate (varies by airline and fare class)
- Qpoints also earned on Oneworld partner flights at reduced rates vs Qatar flights
- Qantas earning example: 25%–150% of distance flown depending on fare class (not on standalone domestic AU flights)
- Always input Privilege Club number at booking to ensure credit

### Avios Expiry Rule

- Avios expire after **36 months of inactivity** (no earn or spend)
- Platinum members: Avios never expire
- To keep Avios alive: make any earn or spend transaction at least once every 3 years

## Avios Strategy

### Core Principle

Avios is a shared currency across five programs: **Qatar Privilege Club, British Airways Executive Club, Iberia Plus, Finnair Plus, Aer Lingus AerClub**. All transfer 1:1 instantly between programs. Earn in one, redeem in another for better rates.

### Avios Value Benchmarks

- **Economy redemptions:** ~1.0–1.5 cents per Avios
- **Qsuites Business Class (off-peak):** 3–5+ cents per Avios — best value in the program
- **Non-flight redemptions (hotels, merchandise, duty free):** ~0.5 cents — avoid unless necessary

### Top Sweet Spot Redemptions (Qatar Privilege Club)

| Route / Use Case | Avios Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Qsuites (Business) DOH–US/Europe (off-peak) | ~70,000 one-way | Best premium cabin value in the world |
| Qsuites (Business) DOH–US/Europe (peak) | ~94,500 one-way | Still good value vs paid ticket |
| Short-haul partner economy (<650 miles) | 9,500 one-way | Great for regional hops |
| Short-haul partner business (<650 miles) | 20,000 one-way | Good value for lie-flat on short routes |
| Economy upgrade to Business (DOH–NYC) | 37,000–55,000 | Only worth it on deeply discounted economy fares |
| JAL First Class (Tokyo region) | ~80,000–115,000 | World-class product, strong availability |

### When to Book Through Qatar vs Transfer to British Airways

- **Book through Qatar Privilege Club:** Partner award rates are now aligned with BA. Qatar has lower cancellation fees (free for Gold+, up to 24hrs before flight). Better for short-haul partner routes.
- **Transfer to British Airways:** Better for online booking of routes that aren't bookable online through Qatar. BA allows more partner flights to be booked online.
- **Transfer to Iberia:** Best for short-haul Europe redemptions (Iberia has cheaper rates on short EU routes)

**Agent note:** Qatar regularly runs 20–30% transfer bonuses from bank partners. Check for active bonuses before transferring.

### Award Search Tools

- **Qatar My Calculator:** qatarairways.com/qcalculator — shows Avios and Qpoints earned per flight
- **Seats.aero:** Best third-party tool for finding Qsuite availability across all programs
- **AwardFares:** Timeline view for spotting off-peak pricing windows
- **British Airways website:** Book Qatar partner routes online that can't be booked on Qatar's own site

### Booking Tips

- Award seats open up to **355–361 days in advance** — book early for Qsuites
- Off-peak Qsuites availability is limited; set alerts on Seats.aero
- Qatar does not publish a peak calendar — use Seats.aero Timeline View to identify cheaper dates
- Cancellation fees: Free for Gold members up to 24 hours before departure

## Status Protection (Keeping Gold / Path to Platinum)

### Current Status Summary

| Status | Oneworld | Tier Bonus | Qcredits/year |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Gold (current)** | **Sapphire** | **+75% Avios on QR flights** | **40** |
| Platinum (next tier) | Emerald | +100% Avios on QR flights | 60 + Avios never expire |

### Gold Retention Requirements

To keep Gold status, SB must meet **both** of the following before renewal date:

**Points requirement (one of):**
- Earn **270 Qpoints** within the 12 months prior to renewal date, OR
- Earn **540 Qpoints** within the 24 months prior to renewal date

**Qatar Airways activity requirement (one of):**
- At least **20% of Qpoints** must be earned on Qatar-marketed/operated flights, OR
- Fly at least **4 Qatar segments** within 12 months, OR
- Fly at least **8 Qatar segments** within 24 months

**Important:** Award tickets do NOT earn Qpoints. Revenue tickets only.

### Renewal Date Logic

- Renewal date = the date Gold status was first achieved (rolling 12-month window from that date)
- If SB earns enough Qpoints to hit Platinum, a new renewal date starts from that achievement date
- Check current renewal date and Qpoints balance in the Privilege Club dashboard

### Path to Platinum (Oneworld Emerald)

- **Requires:** 600 Qpoints within any 12-month period
- **What it unlocks:** First Class lounges across the network, 100% Avios tier bonus, Avios never expire, 2 lounge guests on Qatar flights
- **Gap from Gold:** Need 300 additional Qpoints above Gold achievement level

### Qpoints Purchasing (Emergency Top-Up)

- Can purchase Qpoints at **USD $25 per point** — once every 36 months
- Use only as a last resort if close to renewal deadline
- Better option: book a revenue Qatar flight before renewal date

### Qpoints Earning Strategy (Maintain Gold Efficiently)

- Fly Qatar Business Class on long-haul routes — earns highest Qpoints per segment
- Dubai (DXB) to Doha (DOH) segments are short but count toward the 4-sector requirement
- Use My Calculator before booking to verify a fare class earns Qpoints (some basic economy fares earn zero)
- Partner flights earn Qpoints at lower rates than Qatar-operated flights — plan Qatar flights for status, partner flights for Avios
